## The 2×2 ablation credits the static spatial regularizer, not the video / action-encoder mechanism

The abstract foregrounds the latent **action encoder** ("we leverage a latent action encoder to extract dynamic interaction priors from HOI videos") as the primary technical contribution, with the spatial-aware loss listed as secondary ("**Moreover**, we introduce a spatial-aware loss function"). The Title and intro emphasize the same direction — that VIDA's value is in HOI / temporal priors that static-image methods cannot access.

Table 3 provides a clean 2×2 ablation of (Action Encoder, Spatial Loss). Reading the marginal lifts directly:

- **Seen mIoU.** Baseline (×, ×) = 20.16. AE-only = 21.49 → +1.33. SL-only = 24.58 → **+4.42**. Joint = 28.20 → +8.04.
- **Unseen mIoU.** AE-only = +0.89. SL-only = **+2.40**. Joint = +3.83.

The Spatial Loss alone contributes 3.32× the AE-only gain on Seen mIoU and 2.70× on Unseen. Of the joint gain, SL-only explains 55% on Seen and 63% on Unseen; AE-only explains 17% and 23%. The marginal contribution of SL conditional on AE (28.20 − 21.49 = 6.71) is also larger than the marginal contribution of AE conditional on SL (28.20 − 24.58 = 3.62). SL dominates AE in both directions of the marginal.

Structurally, L_spatial (Eq. 4–5) is a Dice-loss variant whose weights ω_i = (1/|N_i|) Σ exp(−‖x_i − x_j‖² / 2σ²) depend only on Euclidean point distance within radius R_p. It uses **only the point-cloud spatial coordinates** — no temporal signal, no video signal, no HOI dynamic prior. It would apply unchanged to a static-image affordance pipeline.

Two corroborating signals: (1) Table 4 shows 16-frame sampling *degrades* performance below 8 frames on both splits (Seen 18.53 vs 28.20; Unseen 6.72 vs 10.95), consistent with limited temporal-signal utilization; (2) all Table 2 baselines are image-affordance methods adapted to the video benchmark — there is no native-video baseline, so the "video advantage" gap is not isolated.

A control row that holds AE's parameter count constant but ablates the temporal content of its input (shuffled / time-reversed frames) would test whether the AE marginal comes from temporal information specifically or from added capacity / modality fusion. Was such a control evaluated?

Relatedly, "open-world generalization" is operationalized as the held-out-class Unseen split of the SAME VIDA dataset (sharing the GPT-4o + VLM annotation pipeline), not as cross-dataset transfer to 3D-AffordanceNet (Deng 2021a) or PIADv1/v2 (Yang 2023c; Shao 2025), which are the standard open-world references in the 3D affordance literature.
