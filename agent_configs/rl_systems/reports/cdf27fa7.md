The KV-cache compression contribution is framed throughout as addressing "High Bandwidth Memory (HBM) capacity and bandwidth," "high data-transfer latency, and energy consumption associated with KV caching" (Sec. 1). The intro even computes 16 GB for the FP16 cache at n=32768, batch=4 to motivate the problem. Yet the experimental section reports only relative per-token KV cache ratios in Table 1 and Figs. 2–3 — no wall-clock decode latency, no absolute memory in GB, no throughput (tokens/sec), no HBM bandwidth utilization, no energy. The cost claim "StiefAttention incurs per-layer FLOP count and runtime data movement overheads identical to those of EigenAttention at iso-rank" (Sec. 3.5) is asserted by argument (head-sharing for K, projection folding) but never measured. Were end-to-end decode latency, throughput, or absolute KV-cache memory in GB measured at any tested context length? In particular, the per-head value bases (one P̄_{V,h} per KV head per layer) and the runtime reconstruction step K̃ = K↓ P_K^T introduce overheads whose impact on tokens/sec is not characterized.

A second concern is baseline coverage. Sec. 5 (Related Works) enumerates a populated set of direct competitors in the same problem class — KQ-SVD (Lesens et al., 2025), MatryoshkaKV (Lin et al., 2025), Palu (Chang et al., 2025), ASVD (Yuan et al., 2023), ECKVH (Yu et al., 2024), TALE (Lee et al., 2025), ZDC (Zhang & Shen, 2025) — but only EigenAttention is compared empirically. KQ-SVD targets the pre-softmax interaction matrix and MatryoshkaKV "moves beyond fixed SVD bases by fine-tuning orthogonal projections" — both are the most directly comparable methods, and the abstract's framing of "outperforms SVD-style baselines" rests on a single-point comparison. Was a comparison against KQ-SVD or MatryoshkaKV attempted under the same calibration / sequence-length / evaluation protocol? Finally, the layer-output diagnostic in Fig. 6 (5.2% lower decoder-layer error, +3.3% cosine similarity at rK=rV=512) appears to be dominated by a layer-0 spike visible in the middle panel; per-layer numerics or layer-0-removed averages would clarify whether the directional-consistency mechanism actually drives the end-to-end gains the abstract attributes to it.
