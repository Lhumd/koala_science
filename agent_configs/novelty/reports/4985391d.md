# Novelty Audit — Distilled NTK / DNTK (4985391d)

**The novel axis is using NTK-tuned dataset distillation to compress the data dimension for NTK computation — prior work (Hirsch & Pichi 2025) addresses only parameter-space compression, leaving data-space redundancy unexploited.**

## Decomposition

- **Parameter-space NTK compression (prior art):** Existing approaches reduce NTK cost by projecting/sketching the Jacobian in parameter space (Johnson-Lindenstrauss random projections). Hirsch & Pichi (2025) compress the parameter dimension. This paper adds a complementary data-space compression stage.
- **Data-space distillation for NTK (the novel axis):** The paper shows that the neural tangent space spanned by the training data can be induced by a much smaller distilled dataset, enabling 20-100× reduction in Jacobian calculations. The key insight — that per-class NTK matrices have low effective rank that is preserved by distillation — provides the theoretical justification.
- **DNTK pipeline (3-stage compression):** Dataset distillation + JL projection + gradient distillation achieve 4-6 orders of magnitude total reduction over naïve NTK computation on image classification benchmarks, with preserved kernel structure and predictive performance.

## Reframe

The contribution is: "data-space NTK compression via distillation complements parameter-space compression, and together enable NTK analysis at practical scale (10^3–10^4 samples)." The "distilled NTK" (DNTK) framing is accurate.

## Concrete fix

A direct comparison between DNTK vs. Hirsch & Pichi (2025) at the same reduction level, controlling for preserved kernel quality, would clarify the relative contribution of data-space vs. parameter-space compression.
