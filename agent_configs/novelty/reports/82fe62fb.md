# Novelty Audit — GVP-WM: Grounding Generated Videos (82fe62fb)

**The novel axis is the video-guided collocation formulation: treating I2V-generated frames as a semantic prior that anchors latent-space trajectory optimization under a pre-trained world model, rather than using video plans as direct action supervision (UniPi) or as subgoals for MPC (HVF).**

## Decomposition

- **Direct video-to-action (UniPi, Du et al. 2023):** Maps video frames to actions via inverse dynamics model without feasibility checking. GVP-WM explicitly addresses the resulting infeasibility by projecting onto world model dynamics.
- **Video as waypoints + MPC (HVF, Kim et al. 2023):** Uses video predictions as subgoals and connects them via model-predictive control. GVP-WM differs by formulating trajectory feasibility as a full latent optimization problem, not just waypoint connection.
- **Video-guided collocation (novel):** The key contribution is formulating "project video plan onto feasible latent trajectory" as a constrained optimization problem where video frames provide a semantic prior. This reduces the effective search space in the world model's latent space, leading to faster convergence. The formulation is specific enough to constitute a novel method, though it combines existing components (video diffusion, world model, collocation).

## Reframe

The contribution is most accurately framed as: "test-time method that uses I2V video plans as optimization anchors in a pre-trained world model's latent space, converting visually plausible but physically infeasible video plans into dynamically feasible action trajectories." The "sufficient video guidance" qualifier (acknowledged in the paper) suggests the method has a regime of applicability that should be characterized more precisely.

## Concrete fix

A clearer ablation comparing (a) collocation without video guidance vs. (b) GVP-WM would isolate the value of the video prior specifically. Without this, it's unclear whether the gain is from collocation per se (which is known) or from the video guidance specifically.
