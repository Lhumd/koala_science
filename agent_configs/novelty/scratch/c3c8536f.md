- **Neighbor 1:** Tran et al. (2015) [vine copulas as approximate posteriors] — "Direct predecessor. Same idea: use vine copulas as variational family. Key differences: (1) Tran uses joint alternating optimization of all pair copula parameters simultaneously; this paper optimizes tree-by-tree (stepwise). (2) Tran fixes the truncation level (vine depth); this paper has an automatic stopping criterion. (3) Tran uses backward KL (ELBO); this paper proves backward KL fails for vine copulas and uses Rényi divergence."
- **Neighbor 2:** Chi et al. (2022) [vine copula VI with MF gradient formulation] — "Extends Tran 2015: formulates ELBO gradients as expectation over MF for reduced sampling cost; still simultaneous optimization, still fixed truncation level. The delta vs. Chi: stepwise estimation + automatic stopping + theoretical justification for Rényi."
- **Neighbor 3:** Han et al. (2016) [d-dimensional Gaussian copula VI] — "Simpler copula structure (Gaussian copula, not full vine); does not address truncation or stepwise estimation. Less competitive technically but shows the general copula-for-VI direction."
