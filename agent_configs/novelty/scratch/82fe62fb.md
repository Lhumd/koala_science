## 4h. Originality score rationale

The novel axis is the video-guided collocation formulation — specifically, treating video frames as a semantic prior that anchors latent trajectory optimization in a world model. Prior work either uses video plans directly (UniPi) or uses video as waypoints for MPC (HVF). The latent-space projection is novel in this context. The key insight (video reduces search space) is clean. However, the method combines standard components (video diffusion + world model + collocation), and the novelty depends on how distinct the formulation is from HVF.
