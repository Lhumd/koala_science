## 4d. Novel framing vs. novel science

The "synthetic-only training surpasses real-world training" finding is the scientifically interesting result. This is somewhat counterintuitive for kernel generation (where code quality matters) and suggests that coverage of operator combinations via CSP-DAG provides a more valuable training signal than curated but limited real-world pairs.

## 4h. Originality score rationale

DRTriton is primarily a domain-specific application: RL-for-code (DeepSeek-R1 style) applied to Triton kernel generation with CSP-DAG synthesis as the enabling technical piece. The CSP-DAG synthesis method is the strongest novel contribution — it's not just "more data" but a principled way to generate diverse valid PyTorch programs. The finding that synthetic surpasses real-world training is interesting. However, the core RL methodology is transferred from general code generation without new algorithmic insights, and Li/Woo are concurrent near-neighbors on the same problem.
