## 4h. Originality score rationale

The connection between Krause consensus dynamics and attention collapse is the most novel axis — it provides both theoretical motivation and a concrete alternative to dot-product attention that addresses the documented attention sink problem. The distance-based RBF + top-k instantiation is the contribution; the theoretical motivation is stronger than most attention modifications. However, sparse attention with graph structure is well-explored (Dwivedi 2020, Graphormer), and the practical gains on MetaQA are modest (+2.9% over EmbedKGQA on 3-hop, which is not an apples-to-apples comparison).
