## Pseudo-novelty pattern verdict

- **Rebrand: NO.** "Verifier-Independent Curriculum RL" is a precise descriptor; not a renaming of a single prior method.
- **Domain transfer: NO.** Same domain (LLM reasoning RL).
- **Composition without insight: YES, partial.** VI-CuRL = curriculum (VCRL/AdaRFT) × verifier-free signal (RENT/EMPO/TTRL). Composition is non-obvious only in that the choice of *which* intrinsic signal to use as a curriculum filter (confidence rather than reward) is justified theoretically. The variance decomposition + confidence-aware bound (Lemma 4.4) is the load-bearing originality piece.
- **Resolution bump: NO.**

## Expert-prediction test result

- **Pre-result prediction (4f.1):** A confidence/easy-first curriculum filter on top of GRPO without ground-truth verifiers reduces gradient-variance training collapse and recovers performance comparable to oracle-verified RLVR on math reasoning. Asymptotic unbiasedness via decaying retention rate. Probably matches or slightly underperforms oracle on hard suites (AIME). Confidence: high.
- **Actual result:** "Consistently matched or outperformed standard baselines" in oracle setting; "effectively stabilized training, recovering performance comparable to the Oracle topline" in verifier-free setting.
- **Match assessment:** matches prediction.
- **Override:** no override.

## Positioning audit

- **Overclaim relative to evidence: MILD.** "Comparable to oracle topline" is true in matched-budget; "near-comparable on hardest suites" (AIME-2025) would be more precise.
- **Wrong reference class: MILD.** Compares to "verifier-independent baselines" (Majority-Vote, Entropy) but the closest curriculum-side neighbor (VCRL/AdaRFT, both verifier-dependent) is positioned only as a "different setting" comparator rather than a head-to-head ablation of "VCRL's variance signal as curriculum but with intrinsic reward."
- **Missing the natural baseline: YES, somewhat.** The cleanest isolation: (a) RENT/EMPO entropy reward + no curriculum vs. (b) RENT/EMPO entropy reward + VI-CuRL confidence curriculum, to isolate the curriculum contribution from the reward source. Implicit but not foregrounded.
- **Inflated framing words: NO.** "Rigorous theoretical analysis, proving asymptotic unbiasedness" — fine; the analysis is honest, though the bound is straightforward.
- **Buried real contribution: MILD.** Lemma 4.4 (confidence-aware variance bound) is the most novel theoretical piece but is downstream of Theorem 4.3.

## Weaknesses (bullet list with severity)

- **[MEDIUM]** No head-to-head ablation that swaps the curriculum signal (verifier-variance vs. confidence vs. learned difficulty) on a fixed RL backbone — leaves the signal-source contribution un-isolated from the curriculum-vs-no-curriculum effect.
- **[MEDIUM]** The composition VCRL × RENT/EMPO is the precise delta; abstract could foreground this rather than presenting as a unified new framework.
- **[LOW]** Theorem 4.3's variance decomposition (Action / Problem / Masking) is a Cramér/total-variance-style identity — pedagogical rather than surprising.
- **[LOW]** "Comparable to oracle topline" overstates slightly on the hardest AIME suites; per-suite reporting would calibrate the headline.
