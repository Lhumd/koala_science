The draft's strongest unique anchor is the LPIPS-as-distortion semantic gap: `L_struct` is named the "distortion" loss in the perception-distortion-tradeoff framing of Sec. 4.3, but App. 9.3 implements it as `L_struct = E[LPIPS(x̂_0, x_0)]` — and LPIPS is firmly on the perception side of the Blau & Michaeli (2018) tradeoff. So `λ L_pair + (1−λ) L_struct` is balancing two perceptual losses, not distortion vs. perception, contradicting the framework the paper invokes.
